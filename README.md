# SCM Digital Platform

This project was designed for the Syrian Center for Media and Freedom of Expression (SCM), an independent, non-governmental, non-profit civil organization with international presence, including operations and partnerships in Europe.

Due to initially undefined project requirements, the platform was first approached as a news-oriented website. As the project evolved, it was restructured into a comprehensive digital platform focused on reporting, research, and professional support services. The final outcome was delivered as a flexible foundation, allowing future development and expansion by the organization.

##  Project Purpose
The platform serves as a centralized space for:
- Publishing reports, studies, and expert analyses
- Supporting journalists, legal consultants, editors and professionals
- Facilitating requests for legal, advisory, or media-related support
- Encouraging professional discussion and collaboration
